1%TR2ADJ Returns the adjunct matrix, A, based on the homogeneous
 2%transformation matrix, T. The adjunct matrix serves to transform the
 3%velocity from the one frame to another, as described by the homoegenous
 4%transformation matrix.
 5%
 6%Inputs:
 7%-T: The 4x4 homogeneous transformation matrix representing the
 8%transformation from one frame to another.
 9%Outputs:
10%-A: The adjunct matrix that transforms velocity vectors from one frame to
11%another.
12
13function A = tr2Adj(T)
14        
15    R = T(1:3,1:3);
16    A = [R skew(T(1:3,4))*R;
17         zeros(3,3) R];
